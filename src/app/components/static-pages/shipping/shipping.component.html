Delivery and Shipping costs

Free Shipping to France, Germany and Italy

*Unfortunately, we only ship to France, Germany and Italy.

Please allow up to 5 business days for orders to be dispatched.
Our warehouse will be working overtime to ship out an increased volume of orders during sale periods, so please keep this in mind when placing an order as we are unable to make any changes once your order has been finalized.
If you need to update your shipping details, please call customer service on 01234567891 or email clothery@gmail.com as soon as possible.

Express Post is unavailable for delivery to PO Boxes.
Your order will be sent with standard post.

We aim to dispatch all orders within 24 hours between Monday - Friday.
We dispatch all orders received before 12pm AEST (Monday – Friday) on the same business day.

Please note: During peak periods (Sales, Public Holidays, or the day after a Public Holiday), please allow up to 5 business days for orders to be dispatched.

Once your order has been picked up from our warehouse, you'll receive an email from the courier company with a tracking number & delivery estimate.
All orders are shipped with an Authority to Leave – This gives the courier the right to leave your order somewhere safe on the premises if you are not home at the time of delivery.
