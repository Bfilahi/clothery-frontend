Returns & Refunds

Order didn't work out? No sweat, we want you to be 100% happy with your purchase! We gladly accept returns for items in original unworn condition with tags attached. Returns must be lodged within 30 days from the date you received your goods, and we will provide you with a store credit or refund. We've also partnered with Refundid to offer you instant refunds for your returns! Make sure to select the instant refund option when lodging your return. If you have any questions about our policy please send us an email at clothery@gmail.com, and we'll help you out!
